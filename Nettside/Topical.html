=======

Our project is Scan&Sort which main objectives is to make recycling more intuitive, easy, and fun.
We chose this because of the dire demand of having ways to recycle, which stems from high pollution throughout the world today.
This is mentioned in our prior reports: “we have created a situation where 79% of all plastics ever produced is in landfills or the environment” (Geyer et al., 2017).
The way our business makes it happen is by utilizing barcode scanning and AI image recognition with our app.
We also have our website which explains and show our users who we are and what we stand for, as well giving insight into our team and operations.

In this final project we have made our high-fidelity prototypes for the website and app.
Where every iteration of the project was changed with user testing and design theory in mind.
Our newest products are now more visual pleasing and have more interactive features.
For example, you can take pictures of your waste and it show where to recycle it.
There is also implemented new visual aspect with the map and icons.
The website has also been improved with having a guide for the app and the site itself is easier to navigate.
We have also filled the site with more relevant information instead of filler text.
These changes are made in mind with the interviews we had when we preformed qualitative testing.
