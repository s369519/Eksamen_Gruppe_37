Our project is Scan&Sort which main objectives is to make recycling more intuitive, easy, and fun. We chose this because of the dire demand of having ways to recycle, which stems from high pollution throughout the world today. This is mentioned in our prior reports: “we have created a situation where 79% of all plastics ever produced is in landfills or the environment” (Geyer et al., 2017). The way our business makes it happen is by utilizing barcode scanning and AI image recognition with our app. We also have our website which explains and show our users who we are and what we stand for, as well giving insight into our team and operations.
